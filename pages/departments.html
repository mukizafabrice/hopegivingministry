Ministry Departments

Six departments, one mission - to proclaim Christ and see lives transformed in every sphere of society.

Building Godly Men

Men's Fellowship

The Men's Fellowship exists to raise up men of God - fathers, husbands, leaders - who carry the presence of God into every sphere of life. We meet weekly for Bible study, accountability, and brotherhood.

  • Weekly Bible Study - Saturdays 7:00 AM
  • Men's Prayer Breakfast - Monthly
  • Annual Men's Retreat
  • Mentorship & Accountability Groups

Women's Ministry

The Women's Ministry is a community of women committed to walking in their God-given identity, gifts, and calling. We teach, encourage, pray together, and serve our families and communities with excellence.

  • Weekly Women's Bible Study - Thursdays 5:00 PM
  • Prayer & Intercession Group - Tuesdays
  • Annual Women's Conference
  • Community Outreach & Widows' Ministry

Youth Ministry

The Youth Ministry is a high-energy, Spirit-filled community for ages 13-35. We exist to help young people encounter God personally, find their purpose, and live boldly for Christ in their generation.

  • Friday Night Youth Service - 5:00 PM
  • Youth Bible Study - Wednesdays
  • Annual Ignite Youth Summit
  • Missions & Evangelism Trips

Children's Church

Children's Church is a safe, creative, and faith-filled environment where children from ages 3-12 learn about Jesus through storytelling, worship, crafts, and community. Every child matters here.

  • Kids' Worship & Prayer Sessions

  • Parent Partnership Program
