Meet Our Pastors

Servant-leaders called by God, shaped by His Word, and committed to seeing you flourish in Christ.

Leadership Team

Shepherds of the Flock

Senior Pastor

Pastor John Mutua

Senior Pastor

A shepherd with over 20 years of ministry experience, Pastor John is a passionate preacher of the Gospel, a mentor to many, and a visionary leader who has seen God work miracles through this ministry.

Associate Pastor

Ps. Sarah Wanjiku

Associate Pastor

Called to ministry from a young age, Pastor Sarah leads the women's and prayer departments with grace and spiritual authority. Her teaching gifts have transformed countless lives.

Elder & Worship Director

Elder David Ochieng

Elder & Worship Director

Elder David leads our worship ministry with excellence and a deep reverence for God. He oversees the music department and helps set the spiritual atmosphere of every gathering.

Deacon & Youth Leader

Deacon Peter Kamau

Deacon & Youth Leader

With a heart for the next generation, Deacon Peter leads the youth ministry and evangelism department, seeing young people encounter God and step into their calling.

Deaconess & Children's Lead

Deaconess Grace Auma

Deaconess & Children's Lead

Deaconess Grace oversees the children's ministry with creativity and love, ensuring every child encounters Jesus in a way that shapes their entire life.

Administrator & Stewardship

Bro. Samuel Njoroge

Administrator & Stewardship

Samuel ensures the ministry operates with excellence, accountability, and integrity, overseeing administration and stewardship across all departments.
