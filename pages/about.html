Our Story, Our Faith

From humble beginnings to a growing movement - the story of God's faithfulness through Hope Giving Ministry.

Our Beginnings

Founded on a Promise
from God

Hope Giving Ministry was founded in 2012 with a single conviction: that every person on earth deserves to hear the Gospel of Jesus Christ and experience the hope it brings.

What began as a small prayer meeting in a living room has grown into a vibrant, multi-department ministry touching thousands of lives across Kenya and beyond. Every step of the journey has been marked by God's unmistakable hand.
